About Us

MyCircles is the tool that allows you to show kindness and compassion through action. Allowing you to stay connected in a meaningful way.

A comprehensive platform designed to help people organize and manage their digital connections.

The MyCircles app features a customizable interface and advanced analytics tools to help keep people information organized and available.

Personal information evolves throughout life. MyCircles is committed to making it easier to organize, manage and know credible information about all the People in your life.
